# Gemini AI-Powered Lead Qualification Automation

**Gemini AI, Zapier, Google Sheets, AI Automation**

Gemini AI-Powered Lead Qualification is a workflow that leverages **generative AI** and **no-code automation** to prioritize sales leads, reduce manual review, and improve response time. This project demonstrates how AI and workflow automation can optimize lead management and focus teams on high-value opportunities.

---

## Features & Highlights

- **Generative AI-driven lead scoring** to automatically categorize leads as high, medium, or low priority  
- **Zapier workflow automation** for real-time notifications and logging in Google Sheets  
- Eliminates manual lead review and reduces **operational overhead**  
- Provides visibility into lead prioritization and workflow outcomes  

---

## Architecture Overview

![Architecture Diagram](diagrams/architecture-diagram.png)

**Workflow Steps:**
1. Lead enters system (form submission, event attendee data, etc.)  
2. **Gemini AI** evaluates the lead and assigns a priority  
3. Zapier triggers **real-time notifications** for high-priority leads  
4. Lead activity is logged in **Google Sheets** for reporting and visibility
